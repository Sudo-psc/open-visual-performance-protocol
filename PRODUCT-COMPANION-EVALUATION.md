# OVPP 2.1 — product companion evaluation

Compatibility target: *O Custo Invisível do Olho Seco* v2.9.13.

## Product role

The book frames the question and teaches the evidence contract. OVPP turns it into a bounded readiness and feasibility workflow. The open protocol remains usable without paid consulting.

## Release gates

- readiness before data collection;
- validated and licensed instrument with identified version;
- organizational measures never delay clinical referral;
- managers receive aggregated operational information only;
- symptoms, capacity, operations and cash remain separate;
- prospective ROI and payback are unavailable;
- day-90 decision is stop, adapt or advance to confirmatory evaluation;
- null results, adverse events, missing data and conflicts are retained.

## Known limitations

OVPP is a protocol template, not evidence of efficacy. Legal, privacy, clinical and methodological review must be localized. A two-team feasibility pilot cannot establish organizational causality or generalizable return.

## Compatibility decision

Version 2.1 supersedes the former scenario-ROI and scale-at-D+90 language. Any older copy containing “conservative ROI”, recoverable fraction, risk map based on symptoms or a mandatory sequence before referral is incompatible and must not be deployed.
