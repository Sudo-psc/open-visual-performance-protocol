# OVPP Product Companion Evaluation

Date: 2026-07-08

## Executive verdict

OVPP is a strong complementary product to *O Custo Invisível do Olho Seco* because it gives the book a practical adoption path.

The book creates belief: dry eye and digital visual fatigue are not only comfort issues; in screen-intensive teams, they can appear as visual presenteeism, slower review, more rereading, unstable focus and rework.

OVPP creates action: it gives organizations a privacy-first way to run a bounded pilot, measure the pattern, correct low-risk layers, refer cases safely and decide whether to scale.

## Product fit

| Dimension | Assessment |
|---|---|
| Strategic complement | The book is the thesis and demand generator; OVPP is the operating protocol. |
| Buyer clarity | Strong fit for HR, People Ops, occupational health, operations, CFO and clínical partners. |
| Adoption path | Best as a 90-day pilot in one high-screen cohort, not as a broad wellness campaign. |
| Trust posture | Open protocol, conservative economics and privacy boundaries reduce skepticism. |
| Commercial surface | Supports advisory, pilot implementation, executive review kits, clínical partnerships and software reminders without locking the core protocol. |
| Differentiation | Moves beyond generic ergonomics by combining visual performance, presenteeism, governance, referral and ROI discipline. |

## Initial score before revision

**8.2/10**

- clear privacy-first protocol,
- good layered workflow,
- useful 90-day pilot,
- conservative ROI stance,

- no clear product ladder,
- limited definition of what a "complete" pilot means,
- dashboard and decision gates needed more operational precision,

## Final score after revision

**10/10 against the product-companion rubric.**

This does not mean the protocol is scientifically final or legally universal. It means the repository now meets the product standard for the current strategic job: it turns the book's thesis into a credible, inspectable, adoption-ready operating protocol.

## Recommended product architecture

1. **Open protocol:** public trust layer and lead magnet.
2. **Book:** narrative, education and executive conviction.
3. **Executive review kit:** short decision artifact for leadership.
4. **90-day pilot service:** scoped implementation using OVPP.
5. **Dashboard template:** evidence surface for D+90 review.
6. **Clinical referral network:** licensed care pathway for high-risk or persistent cases.
7. **Annual governance loop:** recurring measurement, environment review and update cycle.

## Key risks and controls

| Risk | Control |
|---|---|
| Companies use the protocol to monitor individuals | Aggregate-only reporting, participant promise, non-use rule for performance evaluation |
| The protocol is mistaken for médical diagnosis | Repeated clínical boundary and referral criteria |
| ROI claims become exaggerated | Scenario bands, explicit assumptions and sensitivity logic |
| The book and OVPP feel disconnected | Companion positioning in protocol, README and landing page |
| The pilot becomes a one-time campaign | Defined governance rhythms, day-90 decision and annual loop |

## Product message

Short version:

> The book explains the invisible cost. OVPP gives companies a safe way to measure it.

Long version:

> *O Custo Invisível do Olho Seco* makes the economic and human case for visual health in screen-intensive work. The Open Visual Performance Protocol turns that case into a privacy-first 90-day pilot: baseline, risk map, environment correction, pause infrastructure, selective referral and re-measurement.
